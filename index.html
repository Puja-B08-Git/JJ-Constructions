About Us

M/s JJ Construction has delivered civil works across Pune and nearby districts for over 15 years, with a strong focus on customer service and satisfaction.

For over a decade, M/s JJ Construction has embraced industry advancements and emerging technologies to enhance our operations and capabilities. Our commitment to innovation and continuous improvement has positioned us as a trusted name in civil construction.


We have consistently delivered successful projects across key areas such as water supply schemes, drainage systems, effluent disposal, WBM roads, pumping machinery, and reservoirs. Backed by effective management, skilled manpower, and a strong client base in both public and private sectors, our solution-focused approach continues to drive sustainable growth.

Services We Provide

We are a leading construction service provider delivering high-quality civil engineering solutions across residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects, including fabrication and MEP works.

Infrastructure Projects

We execute roads and external services like rainwater harvesting, drainage, MEP trenches, landscaping, hardscaping, and substation works with precision.

Commercial Building Projects

We specialize in commercial office buildings, shops, malls, hotels, hospitals, and institutional projects with quality construction solutions.

Industrial Projects

We undertake construction of factories, industrial sheds, warehouses, and related infrastructure with a focus on quality and efficiency.

Residential Projects

We build high-rise buildings, bungalows, row houses, and farmhouses, delivering quality craftsmanship and reliable residential construction solutions.

Interiors Fitouts Projects

We execute high-quality finishing works and interior fit-out projects, ensuring precision, aesthetics, comfort, and functionality in every detail.

MEP Works

We undertake complete MEP works including electrical, HVAC, plumbing, and firefighting systems, ensuring safety, efficiency, and technical excellence.
